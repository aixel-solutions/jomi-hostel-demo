Welcome to Jomi

More than a bed - a home between mountains

Jomi Hostel sits right in the centre of Dushanbe, a short walk from the bazaar, parks and the city's best plov. Whether you are heading out onto the Pamir Highway or just discovering Tajikistan, we are the easiest, friendliest place to land.

Clean dorms with proper beds, calm private rooms, a kitchen you can actually cook in and a rooftop where strangers become travel companions. We speak your language - English, Russian and Tajik.
